    Scotch Broom Patrol

    January 7 @ 9:30 am - 12:30 pm

    • « Root in Recipriocity: Tend Together
    • Weeding Fun on the Trail by Yesler Ravine »

    Join us for a restoration work party at Discovery Park.

    Scotch broom really stands out this time of year (especially in the brown meadows) and are easy to pull in the wet soils. We’ve got extractigators, weed wrenches, uprooters and heavy-duty shovels to remove these prolific pests.
