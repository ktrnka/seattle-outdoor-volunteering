Activities

This is a newly adopted forest steward parcel, just down the trail from the Stevens St. trailhead along the Adams Highway section of the park. This will be our third visit since adopting the parcel. So far we've removed ivy, blackberry, and other invasives and continue to remove legacy trash that we uncover. This trip we'll dig into an area that is overgrown with Himalayan blackberry and make piles to decompose on site.

All ages OK

1 out of 15 participants registered.

What to Bring

We'll supply hand tools and have a selection of gloves, but if you have your own that you like feel free to bring those. This will be a light and easy cleanup day to get our bearings and develop longer term plans for the parcel. We just placed a plant order for the Winter so we'll be doing lots of work in this area this year readying it for those plants.

Where to Meet

We'll meet at 9:30am at the Admiral Way & Stevens St. trailhead, right off the north end of the bridge. If you come late we'll be about a 30 second walk down the trail on the left. I'll leave a sign up at the short social trail leading in to the work site.
